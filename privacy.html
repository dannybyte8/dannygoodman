Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Summary

SG Theory Test does not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties. Your practice data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SG Theory Test ("the App"), developed by Danny Goodman ("we", "us", or "our"), handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Usage Data (stored locally): Your quiz scores, progress, bookmarks, and practice history are stored entirely on your device and are not transmitted to us.
  • Crash Reports: If the App crashes, anonymised diagnostic data may be collected via Apple's crash reporting system to help us fix bugs. This data does not include personally identifiable information.
  • No Account Required: The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use its core features.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Device identifiers beyond what Apple's crash reporting uses
  • Payment information (all payments are handled securely by Apple)
  • Browsing history or data from other apps

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we receive (anonymised crash reports) is used solely to:

  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes in the App
  • Improve App stability and performance

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

The App is distributed via Apple's App Store. Apple may collect certain data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or social media platforms in the App.

6. Data Storage & Security

All your practice data (scores, progress, bookmarks) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is intended for use by individuals preparing for the Singapore driving theory test, who are typically 18 years of age or older.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us and we will respond promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
